How to Play

  1. Click "Start New Game" to begin a new game. You will be shown a lineup of six Pokemon that you need to choose, in that order, from the board of Pokeballs below.
  2. Click on the Pokeballs on the board to reveal the Pokemon.
  3. If you choose Pokemon in the wrong order, they will all go back inside the balls and you must start again.
  4. Levels increase in difficulty:
    • Level +: See just a silhouette of the Pokemon you must match.
    • Level ++: Sequence of Pokemon disappear after 4 seconds.
    • Level +++: Both challenges above are in effect.
  5. Keep playing the game to unlock hints and cheats.
